Locations

Blox Fruits features a diverse and expansive world filled with various locations for players to explore. Here are some of the main locations found in the game:

  • Marine Fortress
  • Desert Kingdom
  • Snow Island
  • Jungle Island
  • City of the Sky
  • Underwater City
  • Volcano Island
  • Haunted Village

Each location offers unique challenges, quests, and treasures for players to discover. Explore the world of Blox Fruits and uncover its hidden secrets.
