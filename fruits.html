Fruits

In Blox Fruits, fruits play a crucial role in granting players unique abilities and powers. Here are some of the main types of fruits found in the game:

  • Paramecia Fruits
  • Logia Fruits
  • Zoan Fruits
  • Mythical Zoan Fruits
  • Ancient Zoan Fruits
  • Rumble Fruits
  • Awakening Fruits

Each fruit type grants different abilities and powers to the user, ranging from elemental manipulation to transformation and enhanced physical capabilities.
